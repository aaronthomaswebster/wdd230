Overview

Site Name

The Orem Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Orem Chamber of Commerce website will be a place for people to find out information about the chamber of commerce, its members, and events. It will also be a place for members to find out information about the chamber of commerce and how to get involved. It will have a sign up page for new members and a contact page for people to contact the chamber of commerce.

Scenarios

The following questions may lead a user to the Chamber of commerce website:

  • What is the Orem chamber of commerce?
  • How do I get involved with the Orem chamber of commerce?
  • What are the benefits of being a member of the Orem chamber of commerce?
  • What events are coming up?
  • How do I contact the Orem chamber of commerce?
  • Which local bussinesses are part of the chamber?
